#  How do species interact ? 

I try to understand how the plant-plant interactions are conditionned by their
life strategies, functional traits and environnement.

#  What are the impacts of plant-plant interactions on ecosystem functionning ?

In drylands, the positive interactions between plants can generate positive feedbacks
which can generate alternative stable states at the landscape level. I try to
understand how the local interactions can affect ecosystem properties.
